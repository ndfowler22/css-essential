A first person shooter video game loading screen image

We needed to create an image that would be used during a loading screen in a new first person shooter video game. We wanted the picture to display the chaos that players will endure as they play the game. We wanted the city to be engulfed in flames, but not too much where players feel hopeless in achieving the ultimate goal, relieving the city of the control of the enemies and rebuilding an environment for the citizens.
